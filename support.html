Managing Your Subscription

How to Cancel or Change Your Plan

1
Open iPhone Settings

Tap your name at the top to open your Apple ID.

2
Tap Subscriptions

You'll see a list of all your active and expired subscriptions.

3
Select Maximal Performance

Tap the subscription to view your options — switch plans or cancel here.

4
Cancel or Switch

Tap "Cancel Subscription" to stop renewal, or choose a different plan. Access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Purchases

Restore Purchases

If you've previously subscribed and your access isn't showing, tap Restore Purchases on the subscription screen inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used when you originally subscribed.

Still not working? Email support@maximalperformance.nz and we'll sort it out.

FAQ

Common Questions

What's included in the subscription?
Full access to custom muscle building and fat loss programs, multiple training splits (Bro Split, PPL, Upper/Lower), custom meal plans, nutrition and calorie tracking, progress gallery and body metrics, Strava workout sync, and direct trainer chat with Max.
Will I be charged during the free trial?
No. Your free trial runs for 7 days with no charge. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your selected plan begins and payment is charged to your Apple ID.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly?
Yes. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Maximal Performance and choose your preferred plan. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens when I cancel?
You keep full access until the end of your current billing period. After that your account reverts to free and you won't be charged again unless you resubscribe.
I'm not seeing my subscription after purchasing
Tap Restore Purchases on the subscription screen. If that doesn't work, sign out of the app and back in with the same Apple ID you purchased with. Still stuck? Email support@maximalperformance.nz.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID to request a refund.
Is my data private?
Yes. We collect only what's needed to run the app — your training data, nutrition logs, and progress photos are yours. We don't sell your data. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
