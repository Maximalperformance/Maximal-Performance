Who Is Max

Just A Guy Who Figured It Out.

I'm not a celebrity trainer. I don't have a fancy studio. I'm a 28-year-old from rural New Zealand who started training at 14, inspired by Arnold, and hasn't stopped since.

I went from 64kg to 95kg naturally — through 14 years of trial and error, injury, setbacks, and figuring out what actually works. I train because I work a farm and need to be strong enough to do the job. That changes everything about how you approach building muscle.

"There is no shortcut to building muscle. You have to put in the work — because no one will do it for you."

I built Maximal Performance for men who are done searching for shortcuts. My system is simple: eat enough of the right foods, train consistently, and track your progress so you never fall off.
